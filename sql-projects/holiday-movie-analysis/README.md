# Holiday Season Movie Performance Analysis (SQL & Python)

## Business Context
A European streaming and distribution company operating in Germany
wants to evaluate whether movies released during the Christmas and
New Year holiday season outperform non-holiday releases.

With rising content acquisition costs, leadership wants to understand:
- whether holiday timing materially impacts performance
- which genres benefit most from holiday releases
- whether premium holiday release strategies are justified
